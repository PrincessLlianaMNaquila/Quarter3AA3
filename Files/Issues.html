Issues in Education We Should All Be Aware Of

The learning crisis was made worse by COVID-19 school closures

Several analyses show that the impact of the pandemic on student learning was significant, leaving students in low- and middle-income countries far behind in mathematics and other subjects.

Countries are not paying enough attention to Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE)

At the pre-school level, about two-thirds of countries do not have a proper legal framework to provide free and compulsory pre-primary education. According to UNESCO, only a minority of countries, mostly high-income, were making timely progress towards SDG4 benchmarks on early childhood indicators prior to the onset of COVID-19.
